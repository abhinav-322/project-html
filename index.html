Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, or Murgh Makhani, is a North Indian dish made with chicken cooked in a creamy, spiced tomato sauce. It’s rich, flavorful, and a must-try for spice lovers!

Dosas

Dosas are crispy South Indian pancakes made from fermented rice and lentil batter. They are often served with chutneys and sambar for a savory meal.

Biryani

Biryani is a flavorful rice dish made with basmati rice, spices, and either chicken, mutton, or vegetables. It’s a festive dish loved by many!

Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpea curry (chole) paired with deep-fried bread (bhature). It’s a delightful combination of flavors.

Idli Sambar

Idli is a steamed rice cake often served with spicy sambar (lentil soup) and coconut chutney. It’s a light yet satisfying South Indian breakfast favorite.

Tandoori Chicken is marinated chicken cooked in a traditional clay oven called a tandoor. It’s smoky, spicy, and a favorite among grill lovers!
